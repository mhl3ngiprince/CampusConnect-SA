About Us

Who We Are

CampusConnect is a student-focused platform designed to make university life easier and more affordable for South African students. We understand the challenges students face, from tight budgets to time constraints, and we've created solutions to help.

Founded in 2025 by students, our mission is to connect students with essential services, savings opportunities, and each other.
